Workout 11/16

1. Dynamic Warmup

  • get your entire body warm by doing various dynamic stretches that you're comfortable doing, focus on hips and glutes/booty
  • hip lunge stretches
  • knees to chest - pull one knee up into chest with arms while standing

2. Plyos

3 sets each, rest 45sec-1min bw sets

Coaching Point: focus on crisp, explosive movements. If you feel out of control dial it back a little. If you're tired and moving sluggishly, cut the set short. This isn't a cardio workout so give yourself plenty of rest in between sets.

  1. 15 hops - hop up similar to jumping rope but be more explosive (example)
  1. 10 lateral bounds - jump side to side, landing on one leg (tutorial)
  1. 8 knee tuck jumps - jump up and tuck knees up into chest (tutorial)

3. Full body strength

Super set 1

  1. 15-25 regular pushups
  1. 5 isometric lunges per leg (tutorial)
    • elevate back leg on chair
    • hold lunge position for 10 seconds, and then go back up

Super set 2

  1. 15-25 diamond pushups
  1. 8-10 RDL each leg - single leg deadlift (tutorial)

*Super set - do one movement and then the other one right after without rest. Rest after completing both movements.
